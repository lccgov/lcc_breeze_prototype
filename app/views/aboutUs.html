BreezeLeeds.org is the website for all children and young people aged 0-19 from Leeds (and their families) who are looking for positive things to do. We gather everything we can find out together here in one place and then you can use our online events finder on the home page or scroll through the latest news and opportunities or just browse through the site to find out everything.

Although BreezeLeeds.org is run by Leeds City Council the information included here comes from all sorts of organisations and partners working positively with children, young people and their families in Leeds, not just the Council.

You can be confident that we have checked the quality of the activities and the qualifications of the organisations to provide events and and activities for children and young people in advance. Everyone who posts information on the website is an active member of BreezeCultureNetwork.org and has already provided information to us about their safeguarding and equality policies and procedures.

Breeze also organises lots of events - like Breeze on Tour, Breeze Arts Festival, Mini-Breeze, Breeze Has Talent, Breeze Friday Night and Saturday Night Projects. 

Get involved with Breeze

Check out the online event finder on our home page to find out what's going on near you - or anywhere across Leeds.

Get your free BreezeCard.  It’s your library card, Breeze events ticket, discount card and the key to all things Breeze. You'll get fast track entry to Breeze events, and events organised by our partners, and priority or access to promotions.

Get down to the scorching Breeze on Tour and Mini Breeze events during your school holidays.

Get involved with Breeze Arts Festival; get creative with showcases and performances for and by the young people of Leeds.

Tell Breeze what you think on our contact us page.

Share your information on Breeze

Breeze can help organisations share information with children and young people. We can your activities in the event search section, share your news and photos or spread the word about offering vouchers and discounts to BreezeCard holders. You can also talk to other organisations working with children and young people in Leeds and further afield in Yorkshire & Humber. All you need to do is sign up to BreezeCultureNetwork.org and the whole Breeze world is your oyster.

We can also help organisations with consultation with young people and help young people have their say about things that matter to them. Email us at breeze@leeds.gov.uk to find out more.

Host a Breeze event or hire our facilities and equipment

The Breeze Team offers a wide range of event equipment to hire, including a variety of exciting packages. Our services include:

Hire of our action adventure inflatables.

Access to our database of approved artist practitioners.

Generator hire.

Marquee, table and chair hire.

Videobooth for consultation excercises.

Full, personal, event management expertise.

All events fully staffed by trained sport and youth support staff.

We'll work with you to  really understand your event needs, making sure it is fun and safe!

Our Hire Packages

We have a variety of hire packages to offer, our two most popular are:

Mini Breeze - this is a fantastic event for under 19s in local areas, kitted out with our infltables, arts and music van, arts and crafts and information stalls.

The Ultimate Team Challenge - a fantastic event, perfect for any school to assist in transitition into high school or into sixth form. It is also works well as a reward package for pupils at the end of year. Download a leaflet here.

Alternatively we can work with you to build a bespoke event, we can help enhance a local fate, community gala, fun day and more. Select from our 'pick n mix' range and choose what equipment suits your needs. We have over 24 different exciting inflatables, not to mention our Cage Football and Arts and Music Vans.
